# Design System Documentation

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our user interface.

## Theme Overview

Our design theme is set to `dark` mode, providing a modern and comfortable viewing experience, especially in low-light environments.

## Colors

The color palette is derived to create a vibrant and cohesive visual identity.

*   **Primary Color**: `#3794FF` - A bright, energetic blue, ideal for primary actions and brand emphasis.
*   **Secondary Color**: `#EBCB8B` - A warm, supporting yellow, used for less prominent elements and secondary information.
*   **Tertiary Color**: `#F44747` - A striking red accent, suitable for highlights, errors, or badges.
*   **Neutral Color**: `#1E1E1E` - A deep, dark gray serving as the base for backgrounds and foundational surfaces, ensuring readability in dark mode.

## Typography

Our typography system is designed for clarity and consistency across all content.

*   **Headline Font**: `Inter` - Used for all headings and prominent text, offering modern readability.
*   **Body Font**: `Inter` - Applied to all body text, ensuring a consistent and legible reading experience.
*   **Label Font**: `Space Grotesk` - Employed for labels and interactive elements, providing a distinct and functional appearance.

## Shape and Form

The geometry of our UI elements contributes to the overall feel.

*   **Roundedness**: `1` (Subtle roundedness) - Corners of UI elements have a slight curve, balancing a modern aesthetic with clear distinction.

## Spacing

The approach to spacing influences the layout's density and readability.

*   **Spacing**: `2` (Normal) - A balanced amount of whitespace is used throughout the interface, creating an organized and comfortable layout without feeling too dense or overly spacious.
